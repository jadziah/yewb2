Discover the life of young people in Armenia!



Dear colleagues,

Once again, Youth Exchanges without Borders is organizing a youth project in the South Caucasus. Over six days (+2 travel days), we will explore the lives of young adults in Armenia, with a spe-cial focus on globalization and its impact on youth perspectives. As part of the project, we will visit various institutions on site, including a youth cen-ter, an organic farm, and an export-oriented com-pany.
